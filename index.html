Reaching something by design or default

All (IT) strategies are a variation of

  • Call to action - essence of any IT strategy is delivered by people through process and technology
  • People - an organisation can only move as fast as its capabilities - Agile, ReOrg, Rightsize, Target Operating Model, RACI stuff
  • Process - the ways to be efficient and effective are to simplify, standardise, centralise and automate - ITIL, CI/CD, DevSecOps, GitOps, IaaS, Platform, FinOPs stuff
  • Technology - combine different types of technology to create a compeling user experience - Legacy, Cloud, CORPS, AI and API stuff
  • Totally miss communicating the valour - worth - purpose - WHY to the customer and WHAT time and effort needs to be spent on learning, implementing and maintaining new stuff by the actual people already maintaining HOW live production systems remain up, resilient, secure and delivering a service that customers consider as valuable or of worth to them.
